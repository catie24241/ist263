Social Media Discovery

Google, Snapchat, and tons of other social media platforms are taking over with their “Discovery Stories.” This is my take on this called “Top 10 Book Tok Must Reads.” Book Tok is a trend taking over Tik Tok where users talk and hype up their favorite books and authors. Book tok is a welcoming and fast-paced place full of different types of books, authors, and readers. I combined my passion for reading, motion design, and social media to guide viewers to some of the best books that are loved on Tik Tok. I am slowly working through this list, and I highly suggest you do too!

Deeper Dive into Each of the Books:


The Seven Husbands of Evelyn Hugo

This historical fiction book by Taylor Jenkins Reid is perfect for a lover of old Hollywood and drama! Dive into the life of Hollywood movie icon Evelyn Hugo. This heartbreakingly beautiful story details the realities of truth and life as a woman.


A Court of Roses and Thorns

ACOTAR’s fantasy world by Sarah J. Mass is perfect for any fantasy lover. Explore the world of fairies and ancient creatures a wall away from the human world. This book takes you to a new place of magic, ancient curses, and love.


People We Meet on Vacation

This is a great easy fictional romance by Emily Henry that will sweep you off your feet. The romance flows perfectly and will make your heart smile and convince you to take a trip to the beach. Immerse yourself in the complicated lives of two best friends struggling to understand the meaning of their relationship.


From Blood and Ash

This book calls out to all of our Vampire Diaries lovers. The tale of two rivaling people and a girl hidden from the outside world will captivate your imagination. Jennifer L. Armentrout’s evil creatures, strong women, and handsome heroes will hook you in.


It Ends with Us

This emotional fiction drama explores the highs and lows of any new relationship. Colleen Hoover’s storyline will make you fall in love with the characters, and feel their pain. This is a great novel for strong and independent women or anyone who needs a good cry.


The Song of Achilles

This novel is perfect for the history buffs who can admire a beautiful retelling of ancient heroes. Madeline Miller’s story follows legendary ancient Greece heroes during the Trojan War from a new romantic and empathetic view.


We Were Liars

This fictional thriller follows the chaos and deceit of four best friends and a journey to the truth while hiding on a private island. Lovers of mystery, love, and thrill will be enthralled by E. Lockhart’s novel We Were Liars.


Dance of Thieves

This story by Mary E. Pearson depicts the tale of a Prince in a Kingdom on the brink of war, and a young pocket thief finding her place in the world. The clash of the two characters will captivate your excitement and display the beauty of young and new love.


Malibu Rising

This compelling drama by Taylor Jenkins Reid follows the biggest party of the year thrown by the famous family of the Riva siblings. When a fire erupts, the siblings are forced to decide how they will control the truth, and who will find out.


The Vanishing Half

Barack Obama’s favorite novel of 2020 depicts the confusing life of twin girls who ran away from home by Brit Bennet. While the main characters struggle to find their identities in a world of black and white.
